Detailed Description

This service provides access to a SGP4 propagator.

The SGP4 Propagation Service can be used by an antenna controller or other devices to determine the location of the session's satellite of interest.

Note
This service returns an azimuth in the range [0, 360] and an elevation in the range [0, 180]. If your antenna controller requires an azimuth in the range [-180, 180], you will need to manually convert it in your antenna controller driver. If your antenna controller can't cover the full elevation range, you will need to disable flip pass support using the 'flip_pass_support' configuration setting.
